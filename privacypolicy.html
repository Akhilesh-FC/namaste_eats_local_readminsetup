Privacy Policy for NamasteEats

Effective Date: 1 Nov 2025
Last Updated: 12 Nov 2025

NamasteEats (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates the mobile application and website Namasteats.com (collectively referred to as the “Service”).
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our app or website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use NamasteEats:

a. Personal Information

  • Name, phone number, email address
  • Delivery address and location
  • Payment details (processed securely through third-party gateways)

b. Usage Information

  • App usage data, device type, operating system, and app version
  • Log files such as IP address, access time, and error reports

c. Location Information

  • Your precise or approximate location to show nearby restaurants, enable delivery tracking, and improve order accuracy.
  • Location data may be collected in the background (even when the app is closed) to support live delivery updates and ensure timely order delivery.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver your food orders
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send order updates, offers, and notifications
  • Prevent fraudulent transactions and maintain security
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing of Information

We may share your data with:

  • Restaurant partners to fulfill your orders
  • Delivery agents for pickup and delivery coordination
  • Payment gateways for secure payment processing
  • Third-party service providers for analytics, hosting, or marketing (who follow strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Law enforcement authorities when required by law

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no online platform is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Withdraw location or notification permissions anytime from your device settings
  • Contact us to deactivate your account

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve our website’s performance
  • Analyze usage and traffic patterns

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Links

Our app or website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party websites or apps.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All changes will be posted on this page with the updated “Effective Date.”

Applicability and Scope

Founder Code Technology Private Limited Limited (Formerly known as namasteats Limited) and/or its affiliates (“Founder Code Technology Private Limited”, "namasteats," the "Company," "we," "us," and "our,") respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy describes:

  • the types of information that Founder Code Technology Private Limited may collect from you when you access or use its websites, applications and other online services (collectively, referred as “Services”); and
  • its practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting and disclosing that information.

This policy applies only to the information Founder Code Technology Private Limited collects through its Services, in email, text and other electronic communications sent through or in connection with its Services.

This policy does not apply to information that you provide to, or that is collected by, any third-party, such as restaurants at which you make reservations and/or pay through Founder Code Technology Private Limited Services and social networks that you use in connection with its Services. Founder Code Technology Private Limited encourages you to consult directly with such third-parties for information about their privacy practices.

Please read this policy carefully to understand Founder Code Technology Private Limited’s policies and practices regarding processing of your information. By accessing or using Founder Code Technology Private Limited’s Services and/or registering for an account with Founder Code Technology Private Limited or any of its affiliates, you agree to this privacy policy and you provide your informed consent to the collection, use, disclosure, retention, and protection of your personal information as described here. If you do not provide the information Founder Code Technology Private Limited requires, Founder Code Technology Private Limited and its platforms may decline to provide Services to you.

This policy may change from time to time, your continued use of Founder Code Technology Private Limited’s Services after it makes any change is deemed to be acceptance of those changes, so please check the policy periodically for any updates.

Permissible Age: By using the Services, you represent and warrant that you are either at least 18 years of age or have the express permission of a parent or legal guardian to use the Services.

Scope: This Privacy Policy outlines Founder Code Technology Private Limited and its affiliates' practices regarding the processing of personal information concerning individuals engaging with our Platform and Services. This applies comprehensively to customers, merchant partners, delivery / service partners, users of our digital platforms, participants in events, and personnel including employees, contractors, interns and third parties.

The information we collect and how we use it

Founder Code Technology Private Limited and its platforms and affiliate companies collect information from and about users of our Services, which includes:

Personal Information & Other Information (overview)

Your Personal Information: Information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual. This may include data you provide directly, data generated through your use of the Services, or data obtained from third parties, which, either alone or in combination with other information we possess or are likely to access, can identify you.

Other Information: Data related to your use of the Services that may not directly identify you by itself but is collected in connection with your account or activities (device info, usage patterns, etc.).

We collect this information directly from you, automatically (cookies, tracking tools) and indirectly from third parties.

Information You Provide to Us (detailed)

The information we collect on or through our Services may include:

Personal information

Name, address, email address, postal code, password and other information you provide with your account, such as gender, mobile phone number, date of birth, anniversary date, user bio, nationality, website and profile picture. You may optionally provide us this through third-party sign-in services (Facebook, Google) — we store whatever is made available via those services.

Your content

Information you provide through our Services: reviews, photographs, comments, lists, followers, reservations, order history, favourite restaurants, special requests, contact information of people (when provided), etc. By providing third-party individual's information (e.g., ordering for someone else), you confirm you have their consent.

Your bookings and preferences

Details related to events, ticket types, seat preferences, dietary restrictions, cuisine preferences, travel details (e.g., train PNR), loyalty program participation, and any data you input when interacting with features.

Your activities

Search terms, results selected, duration of use, features used, ads clicked, and similar activity data.

Your communications

Communications between you and others via our Services (may be recorded), participation in surveys, requests for features, employment inquiries, and customer service correspondence. SMS messages logged with phone number, carrier and timestamps when used.

Your transactional information

If you make reservations or purchases, we may store phone, address, billing and payment card info to process transactions. Such info may be shared with PCI-compliant processors.

Public contributions

Ratings, reviews, tips, photos, comments, likes, bookmarks, lists — posted publicly at your own risk and may be shared further.

Image / video data

We may capture CCTV at premises and take photos/videos during events for promotional or security purposes.

Contact information

If you upload your contact book, we will collect contacts (name & phone). You must have consent from those contacts to share their data.

Information volunteered by you

Surveys, feedback, promotional offers, contests, beta testing and any optional features where you voluntarily provide info — used as described at collection time.

Data of Minors

Services are generally not directed to individuals under 18. If an adult provides limited information about a minor (e.g., booking a ticket), you represent that you are the parent/guardian and consent to the collection for the limited purpose. We will not create a profile for the minor or use their info for marketing. If we learn a minor's personal data was provided without permission, we may delete it.

Information We Collect Through Automatic Data Collection Technologies

Service Usage & Activity Data

Search queries, pages viewed, time spent, transaction history summaries, error logs, timestamps, referral URLs, etc.

Device & Connection Information

IP address, device type, OS, browser, unique device IDs (IDFA, Advertising ID), mobile network info, language, time zone, connection type and speed, and possibly device telephone number.

Stored information & files

Apps may access metadata and other info associated with files on your device — e.g., photos, audio, video, contacts (when you grant access).

Mobile status

Presence/absence/details of other apps on your phone may be collected to enable platform features like payment/UPI links.

Precise location

GPS location (latitude/longitude/altitude + timestamps) when you use location-enabled services. May be associated with your device ID.

Cookies & tracking

Cookies, pixel tags, web beacons, flash cookies, mobile device IDs and similar are used to store preferences, personalize content and ads, analytics and security. Cookie policy applies — using Services with cookies enabled implies consent. Third-party sites accessible through Services may also use cookies; review their privacy policies.

Third-party links and services

Links to third-party sites may collect data; we are not responsible for their content or practices.

Information from Third Parties

We may obtain info from:

  • Third-party account authentication (Google, Facebook) — we use permissions granted by you.
  • Business partners (restaurants, payment partners), marketing vendors, data brokers, analytics providers, publicly available sources and referrals.

We may combine third-party info with our own to improve services, advertising, and analytics.

How we use the information we collect

We primarily collect your data to provide you the Services that you opt for. Uses include:

Primary Purposes
  • Core Service Delivery & Operations: Sharing with relevant third parties (delivery partners, merchants) to fulfill services, process transactions, customer support, interactive features, notifications of policy changes.
  • Platform Maintenance, Security & Compliance: Diagnose technical problems, detect/prevent fraud, enforce contracts and comply with law.
  • Service Improvement, Personalization & Research: Understand users to improve content/features, personalize experience, analytics, employee training and QA.
  • Marketing, Advertising & Promotions: Send communications (email, SMS, phone, WhatsApp) about services/offers, show relevant ads, administer promotions/contests.
Analytics and advertising

We use third-party analytics (e.g., Google Analytics) and allow ad networks to serve tailored ads. Third-party tracking technologies apply; we don't control them. Opting out of tailored advertising won't stop ads, only personalization.

Social Features

Address-book access can be used to find friends and recommend connections. Uploading contacts should be done only with consent from those contacts.

Recording and Retention of Interactions

Communications (calls, chats) may be recorded for quality, dispute resolution, fraud detection, training and compliance. Recordings may be retained as necessary to satisfy these purposes or legal obligations.

Integration Across Founder Code Technology Private Limited

We may share data across affiliated entities (Blinkit, Hyperpure, etc.) for unified login, cross-application features, support and analytics.

Advanced processing

We may de-identify/anonymize data and use aggregated insights without restriction. We may also use AI tools to process Personal Data for service improvement, anomaly detection and personalization.

Careers

Applicant data (resume, interview notes) is collected and used for recruitment, background checks, communicating about the role and considering future roles; such data may be shared with affiliates and service providers involved in recruitment.

How we share the information we collect

General Information Disclosures

We may disclose personal information in various ways, including:

  • To subsidiaries and affiliates within the Founder Code Technology Private Limited group (Blinkit, District, Hyperpure).
  • To contractors, suppliers, advertisers and service providers bound by confidentiality and limited-use contracts.
  • To a buyer or successor in the event of merger, sale, restructuring or bankruptcy.
  • To third-parties to market products if you consented to promotional updates.
Service Providers

We share info with providers who send communications, push notifications, provide voice recognition, analytics, hosting, surveys, payment processing, etc.

Legal Purposes

We may share information to investigate, prevent or take action regarding illegal activities or to comply with legal process (law enforcement, courts, government demands).

Audit & assurance

Sharing with internal/external audit teams, payment gateways, banks and other stakeholders for fraud detection, auditing and compliance.

Social Networks

Interactions with social features (e.g., Facebook Like) may result in data collection by that social platform; governed by their policy.

Enforcement & Correspondence

To enforce Terms of Service, for billing/collection, and to send account-related admin messages (some cannot be opted out of).

Platform safety

We may exchange information with other companies for fraud protection and credit risk reduction.

With your consent

We may share information in other circumstances where you give consent.

Information Shared with Merchants

When you transact with merchants (restaurants, etc.) via our Services, we share the necessary details with them to fulfill your request. Partners' use of that data is subject to their own privacy practices — we are not responsible for their policies. We recommend reviewing partners' privacy policies.

Payment Card Information

To process payments, we share card info with third-party processors. These processors may store your card info to enable future payments, as described in our Terms of Service.

Data Storage

Founder Code Technology Private Limited may process and retain your personal information on servers in India and/or servers of third parties (in or outside India) with contractual agreements. Copies and backups may remain in backup storage for recovery, legal, fraud and risk mitigation reasons.

Your Rights & Deletion

If you want to permanently delete your account, use the "Contact Us" link (or the contact info below). After deletion we cannot restore your data. Some copies may persist in backups or for legal reasons. Rights may be limited by local law or if requests are abusive/excessive or affect others' rights or legal proceedings.

Payment processing partners are PCI-compliant. However, we assume no liability for unauthorized disclosure due to transmission errors or causes beyond our control.

You should keep your account credentials secure. If we receive instructions using your credentials, we will assume they are authorized by you.
